About Sound Of Geez

Cologne's Tribal Technician

GEEZ has been shaping the electronic music scene in Cologne since 2015 with his signature blend of Circuit Tribal peaks, Tech House drive, and Disco/Funk groove.

Starting as a bedroom producer, GEEZ quickly gained recognition for his high-energy live sets and innovative production techniques. His music combines the raw energy of classic techno with the soulful grooves of disco and the precision of modern tech house.
