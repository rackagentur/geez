About

Cologne-based circuit DJ, vocalist, and producer

GEEZ has been shaping the electronic music scene in Cologne since 2015 with his signature blend of Circuit Tribal peaks, Tech House drive, and Disco/Funk groove. His unique sound has earned him residencies at Cologne's top clubs and performances at major festivals across Europe.

Starting as a bedroom producer, GEEZ quickly gained recognition for his high-energy live sets and innovative production techniques. His music combines the raw energy of classic techno with the soulful grooves of disco and the precision of modern tech house.

When not performing, GEEZ spends his time mentoring young artists and developing new talent through his Cologne-based music collective. He's known for his dynamic stage presence and ability to read and energize any crowd.
